About Us

Breaking Tradition is dedicated in doing nothing but the Lords work. Every year we strive to put together an awesome annual event for his people. We are more than just preaching. There's fashion shows, teaching, small skits, and a lot of deliverance in the house of the Lord. We aim to think outside the box and bring "difference" to his people! Each year we come with higher expectation than the previous year. Our goal is to deliver many, but if only one is delivered we have done our job. The angels in heaven will rejoice with victory and so shall we! We Break Tradition and take risks.

Our History

Breaking Tradition started from my home church Eagle Saints Outreach Ministry. Dr. Sheiler Hatton my spiritual mother and Mr. & Mrs. Gooding, leaders, and mentors of the church. Our learders strive to help members of our church branch out and reach their callings. Not only do they support Breaking Tradition Ministry, they also have given me their blessing to go forth and pursue what God has given me.

About Breaking Tradition

Breaking Tradition is a Ministry that no longer walks in the shadow of old Tradition. We stand strongly on new ways to reach and teach this new generation. We allow the unwanted and overlooked the same opportunity to praise and deliver God's message. Acts 2:17 In the last days, God says," I will pour out my Spirit on all people. Your sons and daughters will prophesy, your young men will see visions, your old men will dream dreams." This isn't the law of man, but the law of our Father. Tradition won't be enough for what's to come in the last days. Matthew 19:30 "many who are first will be last, and many who are last will be first.” How many people are willing to make sacrifices for the kingdom of God? All believers (Traditional or Non-Traditional), no matter how long or how hard you worked during this period of time, you all will receive the same reward "Eternal Life" if you keep Gods commandments. Break Tradition and come forth, allow the Lord to use you in this time. Because I don't look, talk, or act like you doesn't mean I can't help change the world. God has given me (Whitney) the assignment to help Break Tradition while being used to help save souls.
